About Feature-Based Sentiment Analysis

This is a feature based sentiment analysis project on smartphones.

Twitter data is collected and analysed to obtain people's opinion on different smartphones available in the market.

We display the overall sentiment of a smartphone along with sentiment for each of their features.

Logged in users are given recommendation about selections made by similar users.
